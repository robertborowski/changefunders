Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Does changefunders support one time or monthly donations?

A: Both one time and monthly donations are supported.

Q: How many nonprofit organizations does changefunders support?

A: Any legitimate nonprofit organization can be donated to through changefunders.

Q: How does the verified proof of donation work?

A: Once you create an account, your verified donation proof link will be created automatically at https://changefunders.org/proof/[your_username]

Q: What does the verified proof of donation do exactly?

A: The verified proof of donation link proves that you donated in the current month. We will never reveal the donation amount or organization that you donated towards.

Q: Is the changefunders newsletter customizable?

A: Yes, you can customize cadence and topics for the newsletter.
